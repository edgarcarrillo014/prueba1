AMERICAN COFFEE                 
                

You can prepare it in a pot or in your coffee maker of a lifetime. Put the water and coffee and boil it. It is stirred over low heat for a few minutes and will be ready to serve and drink. You see that it is very easy to elaborate. If you like coffee with a light and slightly bitter body, American coffee is your drink. Enjoy it!
