MUFFIN                 
                

In a bowl we put the flour, the salt, the sugar and the yeast that we will crumble with the fingers mixing it with a little of the flour that we have in the bowl. We make a hole in the center, we toss the butter into pieces, the water and the milk. Mix all the ingredients well with a spatula until a dough begins to form or, if you want to save time, with the kneading hooks of the blender. Mix at high speed until the dough has an elastic consistency and begins to separate from the walls of the bowl. We put a little flour on the countertop, dump the dough and knead gently to form a ball of smooth and smooth dough. We pass it to a bowl, cover it with a cloth and wait for it to double its volume. Now that it's no longer cold, it took about 50 minutes. Flour the countertop again, pour the dough, flour the roll and extend it to form a sheet less than 1 cm thick. We cut discs the size we want our English muffins and let them sit on the counter for half an hour. After that time we put a pan with a thick bottom on medium heat (6/12), we greased it by spreading a few drops of oil with kitchen paper, place the circles of dough with space between them, they will grow during cooking and cover the pan. Cook them over medium heat for about 9 minutes on each side with the lid on until lightly toasted                                                                

List of ingredients                           
                     
  • Flour 275 g     
  • Fresh baker's yeast, 12 g     
  • Salt, 5 g     
  • Butter, 40 g     
  • Whole milk, 100 ml     
  • Warm water, 50 ml     
  • Sugar, 1 teaspoon     
  • Oil to grease the pan
