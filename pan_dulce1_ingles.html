KISS                 
                

Add standard butter and sugar in the blender. Mix well at low speed for 1 minute, then rise at an average speed of 5 to 10 minutes until cream. Add egg, vanilla, evaporated milk, coloring, water and beat for 2 min at low speed. Then add Maribel Rice Flour, baking powder and mix at low speed for 1 minute until a manageable dough. We form the figure of 40 - 45gr and put them on the trays. Preheat the oven to 200 ° C for 15 minutes. Bake 12 to 15 min.                                                                

List of ingredients                           
                    
  • 625 gr. Rice Flour                 
  • 210 gr Sugar                 
  • 125 ml Water                 
  • 20gr Baking powder                 
  • 250gr Butter                 
  • 250ml Egg                 
  • Vanilla to taste                 
  • Evaporated milk can                 
  • 5ml Pink coloring
